Classrooms for 2.5 - 3 year-olds

Our nursery school offers specialized sessions for 2.5 to 3-year-old children through our Panda Bear and Teddy Bear classes. Teachers design a variety of engaging activities that gradually encourage children to interact and communicate with others. The indoor classroom and outdoor playground are safe, interesting spaces for our bears to explore and make sense of their world.

Classroom Learning Activities

Our classes focus on social development and learning through play. Each year’s class builds on the previous year’s program in curriculum experiences and activities offered. The classrooms are set up to invite exploration and foster a lifelong love of learning. Our staff is deeply committed to the young child and the importance of early childhood education.

Twos/Young Threes: Children must turn 3 by February 1 of that school year. The focus of this class is on social development and learning through play. Separating from parents, learning to take direction, and being nurtured by people outside of immediate family are important milestones for this age. The building of trust and growing peer awareness are enhanced by a safe environment that provides a foundation for learning.




Joint Classroom Goals

The nursery school ensures that our school environment is safe, healthy and conducive to children’s exploration and independence. Our teachers encourage students to:

  • Explore, try and expand their experiences and interests.
  • Express themselves through language, drama, art, music and play.
  • Use their words to solve problems.
  • Recognize the rights, feelings and property of others.
  • Develop their motor coordination.
  • Increase their attention span.
  • Become more independent.
  • Appreciate language, creativity, literacy and numbers in many different forms.
  • Recognize themselves as competent, interested learners.
