Orientation Days


Discover our unique orientation process designed to smoothly transition your child into the school environment. This one-hour visit, occurring the day after Labor Day, allows both children and parents to get acquainted with the classroom, meet the teaching staff, and learn about the exciting year ahead. It's a special time dedicated to ensuring comfort and readiness for the official start of the school year.


Parent-Staff Communication


At the heart of our school community is the belief in strong communication between parents and staff. Through newsletters, our school website, and direct emails, we keep you informed about your child's progress, classroom activities, and upcoming events. Our doors are always open for any concerns or discussions regarding your child's well-being and academic journey.


Assessment and Parent-Teacher Conferences


Our approach to assessment is comprehensive and ongoing, focusing on observing children's interactions, play, and learning processes. These assessments inform our teaching strategies and activities. Additionally, we offer parent-teacher conferences twice a year to discuss your child's development and address any questions you might have.


Discipline


We are committed to creating a positive and nurturing learning environment. Our discipline approach is proactive, focusing on setting clear expectations and guiding children towards understanding and practicing respect, safety, and care for one another and their surroundings.


Health Policy


The well-being of our school community is a top priority. Our health policy outlines protocols for when children should stay home due to illness and the process for returning to school. It's designed to minimize the spread of communicable diseases and ensure a healthy environment for all.


Curriculum


Our curriculum is built on the philosophy that play is the cornerstone of learning. Each year's program is thoughtfully designed to build upon the last, enriching your child's early educational experiences. From art to science, our diverse activities support all aspects of your child's development. See our curriculum overview for more details.


Ensuring Kindergarten Readiness


When selecting a pre-school for your daughter, understanding how the nursery school prepares her for kindergarten is crucial. Our approach is comprehensive, focusing on continuous assessment, tailored support for each child's growth, and transparent communication with parents. Here's how we do it:

1. Ongoing Assessment: Our educators conduct regular observations and assessments to understand each child's developmental stage and learning needs. Activities in the curriculum are designed to cover all key learning areas, including social, emotional, cognitive, and physical development, ensuring a well-rounded preparation for kindergarten.

2. Tailored Growth Support: Based on the assessments, teachers personalize learning activities to meet the individual needs of each child. This personalized approach ensures that your daughter will receive the specific support she needs, whether it's in language development, math skills, or social interactions, to thrive and be kindergarten-ready.

3. Transparent Communication with Parents: Parent/Teacher Conferences are a cornerstone of our communication strategy. Held twice a year, these conferences provide an invaluable opportunity for you to learn about your child's progress, strengths, and areas where additional support might be needed. The fall conference offers a getting-to-know-you session that helps teachers and families align on goals for the child, while the spring conference provides a detailed assessment of your child's readiness for kindergarten. Additionally, teachers are always available for extra meetings should you have concerns or questions about your child's development.
